## ⚔️ **StarWars Game**

A text-based adventure game set in the Star Wars universe. Command your troopers, manage resources, upgrade your equipment, and rise through the ranks!

**Features:**
- ⚔️ Combat system with troopers
- 💰 Economy and money management
- ⚡ Auto-regenerating energy
- 🗡️ Upgradeable sword system
- 🏅 8 ranks from Bronze to God of War
- 🎁 Daily rewards
- 💾 Auto-save in browser

[▶ Play StarWars](https://knox4545.github.io/StarWars.html)

## 🗓️ **Persian Calendar**

A complete and accurate Persian (Jalali) calendar.

**Features:**
- 📅 Accurate Persian calendar with leap year support
- 🗓️ 12 months with correct day counts (31, 30, 29/30 days)
- 📆 Week starts on Saturday, Friday is holiday
- 🎯 Click on any day to select it
- ⌨️ Keyboard navigation (← → to change month, T for today)
- 🌙 Dark theme design
- 📊 Shows today's date and weekday

[▶ View Calendar](https://knox4545.github.io/Calendar.html)
